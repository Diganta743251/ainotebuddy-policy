Account and Data Deletion Request

If you have used Google Sign-In or synced data via Google Drive in the AI NoteBuddy app, you can request deletion of your account data below.

To delete all your synced notes and disconnect your Google account:

  1. Go to https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
  2. Find and remove AI NoteBuddy from the list of connected apps.
  3. Then, click the button below to email us to request data deletion.

Your request will be processed within 7 working days. No login or verification is required, but you may receive a confirmation email.
