Privacy Policy for AI NoteBuddy

Effective Date: August 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AI NoteBuddy (com.ainotebuddy.app) collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

A. Data You Provide

  • Voice notes and typed content
  • Scanned images (processed locally using OCR)
  • Notes stored in the secure vault (encrypted locally)

B. Permissions We Request

  • Camera - For document scanning
  • Microphone - For voice-to-text note creation
  • Location - (Optional) For context-aware note tags
  • Contacts - For sharing notes
  • Calendar - For scheduling and syncing notes
  • Biometric - For secure access to private notes

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Enable note-taking, OCR, and voice features
  • Store and sync your notes via your Google Drive (if enabled)
  • Display ads (non-personalized, via Google AdMob)
  • Enhance AI features like note summarization and smart organization

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Any third-party services (e.g. Google Drive, AdMob) are used only to provide core functionality, and follow their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

  • Data is encrypted in transit and, where applicable, at rest
  • Vault notes are protected by biometric authentication
  • Cloud sync uses Google Drive’s secure API (appDataFolder)

5. Your Rights and Control

  • Export or delete your data anytime
  • Revoke permissions via Android settings
  • Biometric data stays 100% on your device

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following services which may collect limited data under their own terms:

  • Google (Drive, Firebase, Play Services)
  • AdMob (for ads)

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. We’ll notify you via the app or our website when changes occur.
